About us!

From a renovated gas station in Burlington, Vermont, to far-off places with names we sometimes mispronounce, the journey that began in 2010 with 1 guy and the ice cream business they built is as legendary as the ice cream is euphoric.


2010

With a $5 correspondence course in ice cream-making from Penn State and a $12,000 investment ($4,000 of it borrowed), Cow Cream open their first ice cream scoop shop in a renovated gas station in Burlington, Vermont.


2012

Cow Cream's ice cream is used to build "the world's largest ice cream sundae" in St. Albans, Vermont; the sundae weighs 27,102 pounds


2015 - Celebrating 5 years

Cow Cream kicks off the year with a birthday bash at our factory in Waterbury VT at the first-ever Winter Fest. Still crazy after 5 years.


2017

Now, Cow Cream is stronger than ever, and the ice cream just won't stop getting better.
